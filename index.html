Established in 2004, Era Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. has been a pioneer in the pharmaceutical industry, consistently setting new standards in the field of healthcare.

From the very start, Era Pharmaceuticals has been committed to delivering exceptional quality and therapeutic value in every product we offer. We take immense pride in our continuous success in introducing innovative healthcare solutions, making a meaningful difference in people's lives.

Specializing in Women’s Health Care, Bone and Joints Care, Skin and Hair Care, and overall Health Care, our diverse portfolio is designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers. What sets us apart is our pioneering spirit – we are the first in India to introduce GLM (Green Lipped Mussel extract) and Emu oil formulations. Additionally, we are among the first few in the country to bring forth L-Arginine Gel/Sachets and L-Glutamine Sachets, showcasing our commitment to pushing the boundaries of pharmaceutical innovation.

At Era Pharmaceuticals, we are not just a company; we are a commitment to your well-being. With a relentless pursuit of excellence and a passion for innovation, we stand at the forefront of healthcare, ensuring a healthier and happier tomorrow for everyone we serve.
